Us OF THE ROAD.
Lights and Shape.
Lights and or dayshapes are to be displayed on all vessels at all times.
No conflicting lighting other than that which is prescribed should be on display on any vessel.
Powerdriven vessels usually display masthead side and stern lighting.
Pincushion vessels have to display a flashing yellow light to indicate that they are in motion.
WIG craft have to display a flashing red light to indicate surface activity.
The Rule has prescribed for visibility of lights depending on the size of the vessel.
Tugs display either two or three masthead lights depending on the length of the tow.
When pushing other vessels tugs display a single white starlight only.
