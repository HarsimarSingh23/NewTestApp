Watchkeeping in Clear Weather.
The Officer of the Watch must take frequent and accurate compass bearings of approaching.
Ships to check for risk of collision. Care should be taken in cases of very large ships tows and.
Ships at close range. The said Officer shall also take early and positive action to prevent.
A collision and check the effectiveness of such actions.
Radar practice should be carried out by the Officer of the Watch in clear weather.
