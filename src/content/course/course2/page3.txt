RULES OF THE ROAD.
Steering and Sailing Rules Section One.
Rule Five Lookouts and Watchkeeping coned.
Lookout coned.
The Lookout Man.
On all but the smallest vessels a seaman should be posted on.
Lookout duty from dusk to dawn and by day when visibility is.
Restricted. A proper lookout is necessary for safe watchkeeping.
A good lookout is usually posted forward on the forecastle unless.
Weather conditions prohibit it with constant communication to the.
Bridge. This is done to prevent his attention being distracted by.
Conversation and activities taking place on the bridge.
