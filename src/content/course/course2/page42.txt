Rule Six Safe Speed.
Safe Speed.
Click for Rule.
Every vessel shall proceed at a safe speed at all times so that she can take proper and effective action.
To stop or change direction appropriately to avoid a collision.
The term safe speed replaces the term moderate speed which was used to control speed of vessels in.
Restricted visibility. Since there was no term for clear visibility this one was coined which was applicable.
In all conditions.
The term safe is relative. The Captain and officers of the vessel are responsible to determine what a.
Safe speed is pertaining to various factors such as.
The size of the ship.
The visibility and background lighting conditions.
The physical features of the sea and the surroundings.
The traffic area and density the vessel finds herself in.
The maneuverability and.
The presence or absence of radar.
