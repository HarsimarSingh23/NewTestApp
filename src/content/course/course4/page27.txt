Rule Ten Traffic Separation Schemes coned.
Click for Rule.
A vessel is said to be using a traffic separation scheme when she is within the bounds of the scheme not.
Crossing the lanes and not fishing in the separation zone.
B. A vessel using a traffic separation scheme shall.
Enter the appropriate traffic lane at the start at as small an angle as possible.
Move in the same direction as other ships in the lane.

Keep clear of a traffic separation line or zone and leave a traffic lane at as small an angle to the.
General direction of traffic flow as practicable.
