Passing Secured Ships Alongside.
Pass as slowly as possible to reduce the suction between your vessel and the other. Higher speeds.
Will cause vessels to surge at their moorings and damage both the moored vessels their lines and the.
Morning points on the harbor.
Secured vessels should ensure that.
Their mooring lines are at optimum adjustment to avoid surging or parting.
