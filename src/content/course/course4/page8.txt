Steering and Sailing Rules Section One coned.
Rule Nine Narrow Channels coned.
F. On approaching a bend in a narrow channel where other vessels may be.
Click for Rule.
Obscured every vessel must sound one prolonged blast on her whistle. If another replying blast is.
Heard it means there is another vessel around the bend.
Click for subtle.
Extreme care and caution should be taken when navigating such bends whether or not an approaching.
Vessel is heard.
A powerdriven vessel must not get onto the wrong side of the fairway.
When two powerdriven vessels are approaching a bend in a river the one with the tide against her must.
Wait till the other passes.
