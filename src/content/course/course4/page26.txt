Traffic Separation Schemes.
Are adopted by the limo and pertain to separation of opposing streams of traffic.
Is a routeing measure to improve navigational safety.
Is mostly adopted in areas of high traffic density when tearoom is restricted depth is limited or.
Navigation is obstructed in any way.
