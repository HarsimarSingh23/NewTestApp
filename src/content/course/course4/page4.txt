Rule Nine Narrow Channels coned.

C. No vessel engaged in fishing shall impede the passage of any other vessel in a narrow channel or.
Fairway but fishing is permitted if the narrow channel is not being used.

Fishing vessels can continue fishing just as long as they get an early indication of the approach of other.
Vessels which will enable them to clear the channel within sufficient time to allow the other vessel to pass.
Through or across.
