Rules of the road
Steering and Sailing Rules - Section One (contd.)

Rule Ten Traffic Separation Schemes (contd.)

d. 	i. Vessels should not normally use inshore traffic zones, unless they are less than 20 mtrs. long, sailing or
fishing vessels.

	ii. However, vessels may use the inshore traffic zones when they are entering or leaving a port or any
place situated within this zone; and also to avoid immediate danger.
