Steering and Sailing Rules - Section One (contd.)

Rule Nine: Narrow Channels (contd.)

e. Vessels overtaking each other in a narrow channel may do so by mutual agreement but both must indicate their intentions to each other through sound signals.

The vessel overtaking should sound a signal first and if it is safe to overtake the vessel being overtaken should sound another signal in agreement. More about sound signals in Rule.

Also reduce speed to decrease the period of running closely parallel to each other.
