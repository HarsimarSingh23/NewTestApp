Rule Ten Traffic Separation Schemes (contd.)

Traffic Separation Schemes – more observations
Separation Zones :

● are created between traffic lanes to separate traffic moving in either direction.

● could also separate a traffic lane and an inshore zone.

● could be used by vessels engaged in fishing, or in cases of emergency.

● should be crossed at 90° or an angle close to it.

● ship's heading should be 90° to the general direction of the flow.
