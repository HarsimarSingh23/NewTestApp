Rules of the road
Steering and Sailing Rules - Section One (contd.)

Rule Ten Traffic Separation Schemes (contd.)

a. This Rule applies to Traffic Separation Schemes adopted by any government or state and does not
absolve any vessel of her obligation under any other Rule.

Traffic Separation Schemes

● are adopted by the IMO and pertain to separation of opposing streams of traffic.

● is a routeing measure to improve navigational safety.

● is mostly adopted in areas of high traffic density, when sea-room is restricted, depth is limited or
navigation is obstructed in any way.
