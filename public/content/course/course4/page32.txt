Steering and Sailing Rules - Section One (contd.)

Rule Ten Traffic Separation Schemes (contd.)

f.  While navigating near the start and end of terminations of traffic separation schemes extreme
caution should be exercised.

g. All vessels should avoid anchoring in a traffic separation scheme or in areas near its terminations.

h. A vessel not using a traffic separation scheme shall avoid it by as wide a margin as is practicable.

i. Fishing vessels should stay out of the traffic separation scheme and shall not impede the passage of any
vessel following a traffic lane.

j. Vessels less than 20 mtrs. in length or a sailing vessel shall take care to allow safe passage of a power-
driven vessel following a traffic lane.
