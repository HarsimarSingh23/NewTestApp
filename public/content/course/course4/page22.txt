Rule Ten Traffic Separation Schemes coned.
Using Traffic Separation Schemes.
CAUTION Proceed only in the approved direction of the traffic separation scheme while navigating it.
Failure to do so increases the risk of collision and ships found guilty of violating this rule MAY BE.
REPORTED TO THEIR RESPECTIVE GOVERNMENTS AND PROSECUTED.
A special international twoletter signal ya You appear not to be complying with the traffic separation.
Scheme has been created for use in these schemes. Any Captain receiving such a signal should.
Immediately check his course and position and make all possible attempts to correct any variance found.
