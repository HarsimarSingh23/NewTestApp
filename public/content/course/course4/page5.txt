Rules of the road
Steering and Sailing Rules - Section One (contd.)

Rule Nine: Narrow Channels (contd.)

d. Vessels must not cross a narrow channel if they will impede the passage of a larger vessel which can navigate only within the channel.

The Rules for Crossing apply here vessels which can navigate outside the channel must steer clear of those that cannot. Reduce speed in compliance with Rule 8(e).

Sound five short blasts of the ships whistle if the intentions of the other vessel in the channel are not clear.
Rule 34(d).
