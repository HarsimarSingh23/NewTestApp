Rule Ten Traffic Separation Schemes (contd.)

Traffic Separation Schemes – some observations

Inshore Traffic Zones :
	● are established to keep coastal shipping away from others traversing the traffic separation scheme.

	● are used by vessels proceeding to or from a port or offshore installation within the zone.

	● should not be used by vessels over 20 mtrs. long, except to avoid immediate danger or in severe
weather conditions.
