Rule Ten Traffic Separation Schemes (contd.)

Definitions

The General Provisions on Ships Routeing defines the following terms.

Traffic Separation Scheme (TSS) : A routeing method aimed at the separation of opposing streams of
traffic by appropriate means and by the establishment of traffic lanes. This Rule has been adopted by
the IMO in congested waters with the aim of improving navigational safety.
Traffic Lane : An area within defined limits in which one-way traffic is established. Natural obstacles
including those forming separation zones, may constitute a boundary.
