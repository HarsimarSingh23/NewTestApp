Rule Ten Traffic Separation Schemes coned.
Traffic Separation Schemes and Fishing Vessels.
Fishing vessels may indulge in fishing in the traffic zone provided that.
They move along with the traffic in the zone.
They do not impede the passage of any passing vessel.
Fishing may continue in the separation zone provided that.
The vessel does not allow her nets to trail into the traffic zone.
