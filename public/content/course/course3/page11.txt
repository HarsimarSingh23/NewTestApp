Rule Seven Risk of Collision (contd.)

Assumptions on <bi> Scanty <.bi> Information coned.

This example serves to illustrate the danger of attempting to assess if a risk of collision exists from a small number of observations taken at long range. Errors in bearings of +/- 1 deg. are possible when using <bi> radar <.bi> and small errors in range measurement may also occur.
