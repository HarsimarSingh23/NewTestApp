The British Government has also issued a Marine Guidance Note man relating to the proper use of.
Radar including area. Captains are required to.
Gain and maintain experience in radar observation and appreciation.
By practice at sea in clear weather.
So that they can deal rapidly and competently.
With problems that could arise in restricted visibility.
Collisions can be avoided by.
Continuous observation of the radar.
Calculating bearings and distances of approaching vessels and.
Evaluating the movements of the approaching vessels.
