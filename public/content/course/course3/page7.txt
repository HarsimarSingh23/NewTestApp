Steering and Sailing Rules - Section One  (contd.)

Rule Seven Risk of Collision (contd.)

Resolution A.917(22) Adopted by the limo in includes the following guidelines on the operational use of AIS.

The potential of AIS has been recognized.

AIS information may be used to assist in collision avoidance.

The following precautions should be borne in mind while using AIS.

● AIS is an additional source for navigational information.

● AIS supports but does not replace navigational systems like radar target-tracking and its.

● The use of AIS does not negate the responsibility of the Officer of the Watch to comply at all times with the Collision Regulations.
