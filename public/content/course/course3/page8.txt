Steering and Sailing Rules - Section One  (contd.)

Rule Seven Risk of Collision (contd.)

Proper use of Radar

Proper use of <bi> radar <.bi> to obtain early warning of risk of collision requires that

	● All controls are at their optimum settings

	● The appropriate range scale is used

	● The choice of display is correct

	● A stabilized display is used enabling compass bearings being read off directly and nonblurring of echoes.

On vessels with two <bi> radar <.bi> systems mounted in close proximity it is advantageous to use the relative motion display on one <bi> radar <.bi> and the true motion display on the other
