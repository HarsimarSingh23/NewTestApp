Rules of the road
Steering and Sailing Rules - Section One

Rule Seven Risk of Collision (contd.)

Proper use of Radar

Proper use of radar to obtain early warning of risk of collision requires that

	● All controls are at their optimum settings

	● The appropriate range scale is used

	● The choice of display is correct

	● A stabilized display is used enabling compass bearings being read off directly and nonblurring of echoes.

On vessels with two radar systems mounted in close proximity it is advantageous to use the relative motion display on one radar and the true motion display on the other
