Rules of the road
Steering and Sailing Rules - Section One

Rule Eight: Action to Avoid Collision ( contd. )

f. No vessel shall impede the passage of another vessel and shall take quick corrective action to allow sufficient sea room for the safe passage of the other vessel. This is equally applicable to all vessels when they are approaching or being approached by another vessel. Even vessels whose passage is not to be impeded remain fully obliged to comply with the provisions of this Rule.

The requirement not to impede the passage or safe passage of another vessel applies to vessels 
	
	● In sight of one another.

	● Approaching each other in a way that could cause a collision.

	● In both restricted and clear visibility conditions.

Caution! Take care to observe the movements of the other vessel ensure that she is not taking any conflicting action which could result in a collision!
