Steering and Sailing Rules - Section One (contd.)

Rule Seven Risk of Collision (contd.)

This rule deals purely with ascertaining situations when risk of collision exists. Rule has four clauses to help determine if a risk of collision exists 


A. Every vessel must try and ascertain whether a risk of collision exists when confronted with another
Vessel. If there is any doubt it is construed that a risk does exist.
