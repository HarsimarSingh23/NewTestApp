Rules of the road
Steering and Sailing Rules - Section Two

Rule Thirteen: Overtaking

This Rule deals with manoeuvres of vessels overtaking one another.

a. Any vessel overtaking another shall keep out of the way of the one she is overtaking.

For ANYTHING mentioned in Parts I and II of this section, including conduct of vessels in narrow channel
and TSS, privileges for different vessels such as RAM and NUC, vessels laying pipelines and cables,
sailing vessels, stand-on vessels, give-way vessels, etc., this rule holds supreme.
