RULES OF THE ROAD.
Steering and Sailing Rules Section Two.
Rule Fifteen Crossing Situations.
When two powerdriven vessels are crossing so as to involve the risk of collision the vessel which has the.
Other on her starboard side shall keep out of the way and shall if the circumstances of the case permit.
Avoid crossing ahead of the other vessel.
Vessel A is called the stanton vessel.
Vessel B is the giveaway vessel.
B shall keep out of the way of A.
