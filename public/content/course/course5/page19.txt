Rules OF THE ROAD.
Steering and Sailing Rules Section Two.
Rule Seventeen Action by stanton Vessel.
Click for Rule.
A. When two vessels meet in a crossing situation and one the giveaway vessel is to keep out of the way.
The other the stanton vessel shall progress at her same course and speed.
However if it is observed that the giveaway vessel is not responding to sound or light signals from the.
Stanton vessel when she is no less than twelve shiplengths away the latter must resort to collision.
Avoidance maneuvers to avoid a collision with the giveaway vessel.
