Rules of the road
Steering and Sailing Rules - Section Two

Rule Twelve: Sailing Vessels (contd.)

Exceptions

A sailing vessel approaching another from a direction more than 22.5° abaft her beam is an overtaking
vessel and must keep clear regardless of wind direction. Rule 13 overrides Rule 12 here.

A sailing vessel must also keep clear of another sailing vessel

● engaged in fishing,

● not under command,

● showing the appropriate shapes or lights.
