IRPCS: Rule Two (contd.)

Precautions required by Good Seamanship (contd.)


● When two vessels approach each other at a bend in a tidal river the one with the tide against her has to wait for the other to pass before moving again. More in Rule 9 on Narrow Channels.

● Vessel's operating crew must note the effects of shallow water. Operating crew must be aware.
