Rule Three: General Definitions (contd.)

Vessel Constrained By her Draught (CBD)

The signal should only be shown when the ability to alter course is severely restricted. On passing clear of the area of relatively shallow water the signals must no longer be displayed. A vessel must exhibit a cylinder by day and three red lights in vertical line by night to be considered as cod. A vessel constrained by her drought has some special privileges as per Rule. However she shall when risk of collision with another vessel in a crossing or head situation exists apply the relevant Steering and Sailing Rules.
