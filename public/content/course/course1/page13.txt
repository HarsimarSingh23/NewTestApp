Rules of the road
Application Responsibility and Definitions


IRPCS: Rule Two (contd.)

Responsibility 

A) No-one is exempt from any consequences of neglect to comply with these Rules or exempt from the consequences of any damage caused by neglect of these Rules.

B) Pay due regard to all dangers of navigation and collision. If there should arise a need to avoid immediate danger to your vessel or her crew depart from these Rules as need be.
