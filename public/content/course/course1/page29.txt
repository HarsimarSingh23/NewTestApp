Rule Three: General Definitions (contd.)

Sailing vessel.
Any vessel under sail provided that propelling machinery if fitted is not being used.


A sailing vessel when also being <bi> propelled <.bi> by machinery shall not be considered as a sailing vessel for purpose of <bi> Rule <.bi> (18) responsibility since she could then use her engines to  maneuver as required by the Rules. She shall however exhibit the lights specified in <bi> Rule <.bi> and shall exhibit a conical shape apex downwards.
