Introduction

Why do collisions occur?


A collision-scenario exists

● when demarcations are absent,

● when no 'white-lines' or 'flight plans' are set,

● when no well-defined boundaries are drawn,

● when both parties are in motion,

● when there are no fixed points of reference.


Collisions finally occur because

● it is difficult to know the other party’s thoughts

● corrections are made by one party and simultaneous reflective corrections are made by the other

● corrections are imagined so thoroughly that they cause what they are trying to prevent …
