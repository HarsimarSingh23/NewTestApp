Rule Three: General Definitions (contd.)

Vessel engaged in fishing


Any vessel fishing with nets lines trawls or other fishing apparatus which restrict maneuverability but does not include a vessel fishing with trolling lines or other fishing apparatus which do not restrict maneuverability.


Fishing vessels other than trawlers have their nets on the surface and can easily catch a surface vessel.
As these nets can be miles long they can be a major hazard to small craft. Mariners must keep a very.
Good lookout for vessels engaged in fishing. Trawling means dragging large heavy nets through the water.
