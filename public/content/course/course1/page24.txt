IRPCS: <bi> Rule <.bi> Two (contd.)

Squadrons or Convoys (contd.)

Action taken to avoid squadrons on the port bow will not be treated as an infringement of these Rules, if executed at long range before the risk of collision begins to apply. (More details in <bi> Rule <.bi> 7 on Risk of Collision)
