IRPCS: <bi> Rule <.bi> Two (contd.)

Precautions required by Good Seamanship.

● A vessel underway should keep clear of a vessel at anchor.

● A vessel underway but stopped cannot rely on others keeping out of her way unless she is Not Under command (NUC). However she MUST display the appropriate signals and comply with the Rules.

● When a vessel anchors she must do so without endangering navigating vessels nearby. She must maintain the proper distance from other vessels while anchoring using sufficient cable and a second anchor if necessary.

● In dense fog, a vessel without operational <bi> radar <.bi> should anchor if it is safe and practicable
