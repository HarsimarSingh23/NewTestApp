Rips Rule Two coned.
Squadrons or Convoys.
Single vessels are advised to keep out of the way of naval squadrons in formation or merchant vessels in.
Convoy. Vessels in the convoy are advised to keep a sharp lookout for such vessels and be prepared to take.
Evasive action against collision. More information in Notices to Mariners.
