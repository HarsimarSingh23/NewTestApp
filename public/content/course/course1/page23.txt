IRPCS: <bi> Rule <.bi> Two (contd.)

Squadrons or Convoys.

Single vessels are advised to keep out of the way of naval squadrons in formation or merchant vessels in convoy. 

Vessels in the <bi> convoy <.bi> are advised to keep a sharp lookout for such vessels and be prepared to take evasive action against collision. More information in Notices to Mariners.
