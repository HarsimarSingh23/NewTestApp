IRPCS: <bi> Rule <.bi> Two (contd.)

Good <bi> Seamanship <.bi> 

What exactly is good seamanship? 

It means correct maritime behavior when one vessel comes in proximity with another

Good <bi> seamanship <.bi> involves


● Compliance with the Rules.

● Having a good lookout.

● Keeping clear of anchored vessels.

● Keeping clear of all stopped vessels regardless of responsibility.

● Anchoring without endangering others.

● Anchoring in fog in case <bi> radar <.bi> is not available.

● Vessels proceeding upstream waiting before bends for opposing traffic to pass.
