<h5> Special Exceptions to Good <bi> Seamanship <.bi> </5>

Strict implication of the Rules may not be possible in the following cases.

<h5> Dangers of <bi> Navigation <.bi> and Collision </h5>

A vessel meeting another head in a narrow channel may NOT be able to alter course to starboard as there may be shallow water starboard and another vessel overtaking her on her starboard side.
