Rule Three: General Definitions (contd.)

Power-driven vessel (contd.)

The term power-driven vessel includes all vessels <bi> propelled <.bi> by machinery for the purpose of certain Rules like <bi> Rule <.bi> 19(b) where even a <bi> hampered <.bi> power-driven vessel would be expected to have her engines ready for immediate <bi> manoeuvre <.bi> in restricted visibility.


However a power-driven vessel which is NUC, RAM or engaged in fishing will not exhibit the lights
specified in <bi> Rule <.bi> 23 for a 'Power Driven Vessel', but shall exhibit the lights specified in either <bi> Rule <.bi> 26 or
Rule 27 for the vessel of that privilege.
