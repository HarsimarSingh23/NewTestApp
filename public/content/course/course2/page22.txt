Rule Five Lookouts and Watchkeeping (contd.)

Mandatory Standards for Safe Watchkeeping (contd.)

12. The Officer of the Watch is the Captains representative and primarily responsible for the safe navigation of the ship and for complying with the rips.

13. A proper lookout shall be made available to 
	
	A. Maintain vigilance by sight and hearing for any change in the operating environment.

	B. Fully appraise the situation and the risk of collision stranding and other dangers to 	navigation.

	C. Detect ships or aircraft in distress shipwrecked persons wrecks debris and other hazards to 	safe navigation.

14. The look-out should be dedicated and should have no other duties that will interfere with his duties.
