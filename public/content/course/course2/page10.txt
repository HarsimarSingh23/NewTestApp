Steering and Sailing Rules - Section One

Rule Five Lookouts and Watchkeeping (contd.)

Lookout (contd.)

Full <bi> appraisal <.bi> of the situation 

In addition to what is going on around him the Officer of the Watch or the person acting as the.
Lookout should be aware of what is going on aboard his own ship. He should also ensure that the.
Equipment on his own vessel is operating properly, and keep an eye on the steering and the speed.

In the past there have been several cases of <bi> collisions <.bi> that can be attributed to the failure of the steering gear automatic pilot or gyrocompass.
