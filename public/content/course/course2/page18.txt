Regulation VIII/1

Fitness for Duty

Each Administration shall, for the purpose of preventing fatigue:

Establish and enforce rest periods for watchkeeping personnel; and

Require that watch systems are so arranged that the efficiency of all watchkeeping personnel is not impaired by fatigue and that duties are so organized that the first watch at the commencement of a voyage and subsequent relieving watches are sufficiently rested and otherwise fit for duty.

Regulation VIII/1 - Prevention of Fatigue with respect to watchkeeping personnel.

For the purpose of preventing fatigue every Administration or Government agency shall.
1. Create and enforce rest periods for watchkeeping personnel and

2. Arrange watch systems so that the efficiency of all watchkeeping personnel is not impaired by fatigue and from the beginning of a voyage till the end the personnel are sufficiently rested and otherwise fit for duty.
