Performing a Navigational Watch coned.
The Officer of the Watch shall also.
Make the optimum use of all navigational equipment on board.
Use radar if present subject to the provisions of its use under the rips.
Use the helm soundsignalling equipment or engines if necessary to avoid a collision.
Know the characteristics of his vessel especially stopping and turning distances.
Maintain a proper record of the happenings on his period of watchkeeping.
Ensure that a proper lookout is maintained at all times.
Carry out operational testing of navigational equipment as often as deemed necessary.
