Steering and Sailing Rules - Section One

Rule Five Lookouts and Watchkeeping (contd.)

Basic Principles for Safe Watchkeeping Regulation 1/1(contd.)

7. Navigational equipment.

A. The Officer of the Watch must make the most effective use of all navigational equipment at his disposal.

B. When using radar he must know and follow the provisions laid down in the Regulations for Prevention of Collision at Sea.

C. The Officer of the Watch should not hesitate to use the helm engines and sound-signalling apparatus if the need should arise.
