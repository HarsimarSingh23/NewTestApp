Rule Five Lookouts and Watchkeeping (contd.)

Mandatory Standards for Safe Watchkeeping (contd.)

Performing a Navigational Watch coned.

The Officer of the Watch shall also.

35. Comply with the conditions and standards of the International Convention for the Safety Of Life At Sea solar. He shall also.

● Ensure manual steering of the vessel in good time to avoid any potentially dangerous situation.

● Understand that with a ship with automatic steering he should not allow the situation to develop to a point where he has to interrupt lookout duty to head off a potentially dangerous situation.

36. Know understand and use the capabilities and limitations of all the electronic navigational aids carried as and when necessary.

37. Use radar whenever restricted visibility arises and continuously in congested areas.

38. Ensure that range scales are adopted and their return echoes analyzed as frequently as possible.

39. Select an appropriate range scale while using radar to enable effective plotting and systematic analysis.
