Rule Five Lookouts and Watchkeeping (contd.)

Lookout (contd.)

Ocean Lookout

Look-out duties may be relaxed on the ocean where chances of collision are minimal being a huge <bi> expanse <.bi> with little or no traffic.

Visual lookout however is still necessary.

From various court rulings it has been observed that the duties of a lookout are not to be taken lightly and a lookout man should have the proper and complete knowledge of how to use every audio and visual aid available at his disposal.
