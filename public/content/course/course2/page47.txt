Radar Interference (contd.)

● Minor targets such as small coastal vessels and trainers should be detected at a range greater than 6 miles for effective action to be taken.

● Fiberglass boats and small yachts often give poor reflective images on radar.

● SOLAS requires that all ships of less than gross tons be fitted with radar reflectors to enable detection at both and hz mounted at least mars. Above the waterline.

● Too much reliance however should not be placed on radar.
