The Officer of the Watch shall.
A. Keep watch on the bridge.
B. Leave the bridge only if and when properly relieved.
C. Continue to be responsible for the safe navigation of the ship inspire of the Captain being on the bridge.
Till such responsibility is resumed by the Captain and communicated to the Officer of the Watch.
D. Notify the Captain when any doubt arises as to what action to take in the interests of safety to the vessel.
The said Officer shall also.
Frequently check course steered position and speed as deemed necessary.
Know everything about the location and operation of all safety and navigational equipment including.
Its limitations.
Not attempt any additional duties which will interfere with the safe navigation of the ship.
