Navigation with Pilot on board

49. The Captain and the Officer of the Watch are still responsible for the safety of their vessel inspire of having a harbor Pilot on board. They shall cooperate closely with the Pilot to ensure the set course and speed is maintained.

50. The Officer of the Watch shall clarify any doubts with the Pilot and if one should still exist notify the Captain immediately.
