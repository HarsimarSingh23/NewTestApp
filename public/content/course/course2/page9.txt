Rule Five Lookouts and Watchkeeping (contd.)

Lookout (contd.)

Lookout at anchor

A lookout is also required when the vessel is at anchor especially if there is a strong tide running of
if other vessels are expected to be passing by.

A lookout has to report any anchor dragging to the navigating officers or the Officer of the Watch for further action and rectification.
