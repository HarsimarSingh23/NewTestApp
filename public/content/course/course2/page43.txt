Rules of the road
Steering and Sailing Rules - Section One

Rule Six Safe Speed (contd.)

The following points require to be considered

● Safe speed applies to every vessel especially cod RAM.

● Safe speed may vary according to changing situations and the feedback given by radar.

● Safe speed must not be too high or too low. Avoid high speed in low visibility situations and low speeds in situations where the vessel slowing down is unable to get out of the way of another vessel.
