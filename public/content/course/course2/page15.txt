Navigational Duties and Responsibilities.
Click for Regulation.
A. The Officer of the Watch shall.
I. Remain on the bridge and keep watch until properly relieved.
I. Continue to be responsible for the safe navigation of the ship until expressed relieved by the.
Vessels Captain by verbal order.
I. Notify the Captain when in any doubt as to the next course of action to be taken.
I. Not hand over watch duty to the relieving officer if he feels that the said officer is not competent.
For the said duty he is to notify the Captain immediately.
B. The relieving officer shall satisfy himself as to the ships true position and.
Confirm her intended track course speed and note any navigational danger.
If present or expected to be encountered during his watch.
