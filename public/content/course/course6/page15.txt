Navigation Direction School.
LES OF THE ROAD.
Steering and Sailing Rules Section the.
In restricted visibility conditions vessels must.
Ply at a safe speed.
Have engines ready for immediate maneuver.
When one vessel detects another by radar she should determine whether a closequartes situation exists.
And find ways to avoid it.
A minimum of miles is the distance considered as a closequarters situation in restricted visibility or in.
Congested waters.
In a closequarters situation.
Vessels may alter their course to starboard.
Reduce speed on hearing fog signal forward of the beam.
Navigate with extreme caution till the danger of collision is past.
Avoid continuous alterations of course in the fog.
Reversing engines should be avoided as the noise may drown out further fog signals.
