Rules of the road
Steering and Sailing Rules - Section Three

Rule Nineteen Conduct of Vessels in Restricted Visibility (contd.)

e. Every vessel shall reduce or even take off her speed and maneuver with extreme caution when

	● she hears a fog signal apparently forward of her beam.

	● there is a close-quarters situation with another vessel forward of her beam.

No quantifiable distance has been fixed to effectively determine when a close-quarters situation exists it is dependant upon a number of factors.
