Rules of the road
Lights and Shape

Rule Twenty Three : Power-driven Vessels Underway (contd.)

A towing light in a vertical line above the sternlight
Three masthead lights in a vertical line
A diamond shape where it can best be seen
Sidelights (red for port, green for starboard)

If the total length of the tow (from the stern of the tug to the end of the vessel being towed) is greater than 200 metres.

If the total tow length (stern of the tug to the end of the tow) is less than 200 mtrs., the towing vessel shall display

A towing light in a vertical line above the sternlight
Two masthead lights in a vertical line
Sidelights (red for port, green for starboard)
