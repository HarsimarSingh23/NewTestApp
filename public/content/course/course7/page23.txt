Rules of the road
Lights and Shape

Rule Twenty Four Towing and Pushing (contd.)

Information on the lighting requirements for vessels under specific conditions. It states that if a vessel is wider than 25 meters, it must have four all-round white lights positioned at all corners. Additionally, if a vessel exceeds 100 meters in length, it must display additional all-round white lights apart from those at all corners, ensuring that the distance between lights is less than 100 meters.
