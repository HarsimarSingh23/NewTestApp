Lights and Shape

Rule Twenty One: Definitions (contd.)

f. Flashing light.

A light flashing regularly at the rate of flashes or more per minute flashes or more per second.

Flashing lights are displayed on 

● air-cushion vessels like hovercraft.

● Submarines

The high rate of flashing is to differentiate a hovercraft from a buoy whose lights flash at just 60 flashes per minute.
