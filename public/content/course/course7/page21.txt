Lights and Shape

Rule Twenty Four Towing and Pushing (contd.)

f. Any number of vessels being towed alongside or pushed in a group shall  be lighted as one vessel.

(i) A vessel being pushed ahead not being part of a composite unit shall exhibit - Sidelights (red for port, freen for starboard)

(ii) A vessel being towed alongside shall have sterlight and Sidelights (red for port, freen for starboard)
