Rule Twenty Four Towing and Pushing (contd.)

e. A vessel being towed other than inconspicuous partly submerged vessels objects or a combination of such vessels or objects shall exhibit 

A sternlight is must.

A diamond shape where it can best be seen total tow length more than 200 metres 

No other shapes when the total tow length is less than 200 metres.

Sidelights (red for port Green for starboard)
