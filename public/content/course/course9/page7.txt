Rule Thirty Three: Equipment for Sound Signals

(a) A vessel of 12 meters or more in length shall be provided with a whistle, a vessel of 20 meters or more in length shall be provided with a <bi> bell <.bi> in addition to a whistle, and a vessel of 100 meters or more in length shall, in addition, be provided with a gong, the tone and sound of which cannot be confused with that of the bell. The whistle, <bi> bell <.bi> and <bi> gong <.bi> shall comply with the specifications in Annex III to these Regulations. The <bi> bell <.bi> or <bi> gong <.bi> or both may be replaced by other equipment having the same respective sound characteristics, provided that manual sounding of the prescribed signals shall always be possible.

(b) A vessel of less than 12 meters in length shall not be obliged to carry the sound signaling appliances prescribed in paragraph (a) of this <bi> Rule <.bi> but if she does not, she shall be provided with some other means of making an efficient sound signal.
