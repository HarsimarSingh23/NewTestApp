2. Bell or Gong
Click for Sound

(a) Intensity of signal : Any bell or gong for maritime use should have a pressure level of at
least 110 dB when heard from a distance of 1 metre away.
(b) Construction :Maritime bells must be made from corrosion resistant metal.
The bell / gong should give off a clear tone when struck.
Diameter of the bell mouth should be at least 300mm (vessels of
20M or more).


A maritime bell may be struck by a power-driven appliance to ensure
constant force.
Manual striking of such a bell should also be possible.
The striker mass should be at least 3% of that of the bell.
