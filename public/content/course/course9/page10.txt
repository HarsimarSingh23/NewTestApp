RULES OF THE ROAD
Sound and Light Signal

Rule Thirty Four: Manoeuvering and Warning Signals (contd.)

b. The following light signals may also be used simultaneously with the whistle signals, while manoeuvering :

Light Signal - Manoeuver

One flash (1 second duration) - I am altering course to starboard.

Two flashes - I am altering course to port.

Three flashes - My vessel is operating astem propulsion.
