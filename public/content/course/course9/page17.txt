RULES OF THE ROAD
Sound and Light Signal

Rule Thirty Five: Sound Signals in Restricted Visibility (contd.)

This Rule deals with sound signals to be given in times of restricted visibility.

The following table gives the sound signals for various vessels in varied conditions :

Vessel characteristics - Sound Signal

Vessel being towed / last vessel in tow - One prolonged blast followed by three short blasts, sounded every two minutes.

Composite units (vessel being pushed and the pushing vessel) - One prolonged blast every two minutes / Two
prolonged blasts with a two-second interval between each; sounded every two minutes.
