RULES OF THE ROAD
Sound and Light Signal

Rule Thirty Four: Manoeuvering and Warning Signals

This Rule deals with the various signals (sound and light) given by vessels

while manoeuvering or if one has to send out a warning to another.

a. Whistle signals for power-driven vessels underway, while manoeuvering : 


Sound Signal  - Manoeuver

One short blast (1 second long) - I am altering course to starboard. 

Two short blasts - I am altering course to port.

Three short blasts - My vessel is operating astern propulsion.
