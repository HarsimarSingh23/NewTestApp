RULES OF THE ROAD
Sound and Light Signal

Rule Thirty Two: Definitions

The IRPCS has laid down certain guidelines for sound-signalling devices

to be used on vessels.
This chapter will enlighten you on the various sound-signalling devices, their volume levels and durations
of their use – with the meanings attached to each.
The Whistle – is a device capable of producing blasts of sound – which must comply with specifications
laid down.

Durations are important :
A short blast is a whistle blown for around 1 second.
A prolonged blast is a whistle blown from 4 to 6 seconds.
