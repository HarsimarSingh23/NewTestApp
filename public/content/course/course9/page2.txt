RULES OF THE ROAD
Sound and Light Signal

Rule Thirty Three: Equipment for Sound Signals

This is the sound-signalling equipment prescribed for various vessels :

Sound-signalling device
Vessel description
Whistle or other device like foghorn
Vessel more than 12M long
Whistle and Bell
Vessel more than 20M long
Whistle, Bell and Gong (deeper sound)
Vessel more than 100M long
Vessels below 12M in length need not have the above sound signalling devices, but should carry some
means of producing an efficient sound signal – for example – an air-powered or electric foghorn.
