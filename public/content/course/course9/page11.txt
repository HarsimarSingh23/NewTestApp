RULES OF THE ROAD
Sound and Light Signal

Rule Thirty Four: Manoeuvering and Warning Signals (contd.)
c. When a power-driven vessel wishes to overtaken another in a narrow channel :

Sound Signal by Overtaking Vessel - Intended Manoeuver

Two prolonged blasts, one short blast - I intend to overtake you on your starboard side.
Two prolonged blasts, two short blasts -I intend to overtake you on your port side.

Sound Signal by Vessel being overtaken -Message
One long, one short, one long, one short - Perform your manoeuver
