RULES OF THE ROAD
Sound and Light Signal

Rule Thirty Five: Sound Signals in Restricted Visibility
This Rule deals with sound signals to be given in times of restricted visibility.

The following table gives the sound signals for various vessels in varied conditions :
Vessel characteristics - Power Driven

Status in restricted visibility - Sound Signal
Making way -One prolonged blast sounded every two minutes.

Underway but stopped - Two prolonged blasts with an interval of two seconds sounded every two minutes.
