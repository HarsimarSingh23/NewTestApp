Lights and Shapes (contd.)

Rule Twenty Seven: Vessels NUC or RAM (contd.)

c. A power-driven towing vessel engaged in a towing operation and in the meantime, RAM, shall exhibit :

Three all-round vertical lights, top and bottom red with white in the middle

Two masthead lights in a vertical line.

Two circles and a diamond in the middle, where they can best be seen

 A towing light above the sternlight

Length of tug less than 50 metres; total length of tow less than 200 metres;
