Rules of the road
Lights and Shapes (contd.)

Rule Twenty Five: Sailing Vessels Underway and Vessels Under Oars (contd.)

d. (i) A sailing vessel less than 7 metres long or (ii) a vessel under oars may exhibit the prescribed stern
and side lights, but if she does not have these, an electric torch or lighted lantern showing a white light is
also allowed. This light should be displayed in ample time to avoid a collision.
