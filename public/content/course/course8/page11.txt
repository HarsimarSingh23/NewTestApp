Lights and Shapes (contd.)

Rule Twenty Six: Fishing Vessels (contd.)

Vessels fishing with purse seine gear may exhibit :

Condition - Signal to be exhibited
when fishing - two yellow lights in a vertical line
when hampered by fishing gear - two yellow lights flashing once every second
Vessels of less than 20 metres in length may also exhibit the above signals.

Annexure II
(e) A vessel when not engaged in fishing shall not exhibit the lights or shapes quick prescribed in this Rule, but only those prescribed for a vessel of her length.
