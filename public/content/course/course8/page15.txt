Rules of the road
Lights and Shapes (contd.)

Rule Twenty Seven: Vessels NUC or RAM (contd.)

d. Vessels engaged in dredging or underwater operations, when RAM, shall exhibit the same lights and shapes as in sub-rule (b). When an obstruction exists, they shall exhibit :

Two all-round green lights in a vertical line to indicate on which side a vessel may pass

Day shapes: Two round balls indicate the obstruction.

Two diamond shapes indicate the passageway

Two all-round red lights in a vertical line to indicate on which side the obstruction exists
