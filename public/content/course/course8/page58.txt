Lights and Shapes coned.
Sailing vessels have lighting requirements depending on their size.
Vessels under oars only require a torch electric lantern to be kept close at hand.
Fishing vessels require to display lighting adequate to their status.
Pair trawling requires floodlighting and various combinations of lights depending on the.
Activity on the trawler.
Vessels nut RAM and cod have additional lighting to warn other vessels of their special status.
Pilot boats need to fly International Code H flag to indicate the presence of a pilot on board.
Vessels have to display a special combination of lights when anchored or aground.
Seaplanes and WIG craft have their own combinations of lighting which is quite similar to that.
