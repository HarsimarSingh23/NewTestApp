Rules of the road
Lights and Shapes (contd.)

Rule Thirty: Anchored Vessels & Vessels Aground (contd.)

d. A vessel aground shall exhibit:

Length > 50 m
Two all-round red lights in a vertical line
Dayshape
Forward masthead light, sternlight
If the vessel is less than 50 metres long, she shall display:

Two all-round red lights in a vertical line
Single all-round white light
