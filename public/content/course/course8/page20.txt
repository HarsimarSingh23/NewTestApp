Rule Twenty Nine: Pilot Vessels

This Rule deals with lights and shapes to be exhibited by Pilot Vessels with harbour pilots on board.

a. Pilot vessels on duty (power-driven or sailing) shall exhibit :
Two all-round masthead lights, white over red in vertical line. Vessel less than 50M long.

Two all-round masthead lights, white over red in a vertical line

While at anchor, pilot vessels should exhibit the lights prescribed for vessels at anchor.
No mast head light is displayed when not on pilot duty.
