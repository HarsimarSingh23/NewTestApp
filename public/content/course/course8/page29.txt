Lights and Shapes (contd.)

Rule Thirty: Anchored Vessels & Vessels Aground (contd.)

e. Anchored vessels that are less than 7 metres long do not require to display lights or dayshapes prescribed in para (a) / (b) of this Rule, but could do so.

f. Vessels less than 12 metres long when aground need not exhibit the lights or shapes prescribed in para (d) of this Rule.

g. (US Inland Rules Part 30(g) for Exemptions in Special Anchorages) A vessel less than 20 meters in length, when at anchor in a special anchorage area designated by the Secretary, shall not be required to exhibit the anchor lights and shapes required by this Rule.
