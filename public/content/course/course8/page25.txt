Rule Thirty: Anchored Vessels & Vessels Aground (contd.)

Positioning of Lighting for Anchored Vessels

NOTE : For vessels less than 50 metres long, the forward anchor light must be at least 4.5 metres above the aft one.
For vessels longer than 50 metres, the forward anchor light must be at least 6 metres from the hull.
