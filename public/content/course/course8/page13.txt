Rules of the road
Lights and Shapes (contd.)

Rule Twenty Seven: Vessels NUC or RAM (contd.)

b. All vessels RAM except mine clearance vessels in operation or at anchor shall exhibit :

Three all-round vertical lights, top and bottom red with white in the middle

Day shapes : Two circles and a diamond in the middle

When the vessel is making way through the water, she must exhibit :  Sidelights and stem light addition to the other prescribed lights
