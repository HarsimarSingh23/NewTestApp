Rule Twenty Seven: Vessels NUC or RAM

This Rule deals with the lights and shapes to be displayed by vessels NC or RAM.

a. A vessel not under command shall exhibit :

Two all round red lights in a vertical line where they can best be seen.

Two all round red lights in a vertical line where they can best be seen.

NOTE: NO MASTHEAD LIGHT HENCE NO INDICATION OF LENGTH
