Lights and Shapes (contd.)

Rule Twenty Seven: Vessels NUC or RAM (contd.)

(g) Vessels of less than 12 meters in length, except those engaged in diving operations, shall not be required to exhibit the lights and shapes prescribed in this Rule. However, in 1981, the rule was amended to include lights and shapes to be kept in readiness.

(h) The signals prescribed in this Rule are not signals of vessels in distress and requiring assistance. Such signals are contained in Annex IV to these Regulations.
