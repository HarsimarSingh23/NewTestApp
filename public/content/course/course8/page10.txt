Rule Twenty Six: Fishing Vessels (contd.)

Pair trawlers of 20 metres or more :
Condition - Signal to be exhibited

at night - a searchlight facing forward and over the other vessel in the pair

when shooting a net - two white lights in a vertical line

when hauling in nets - a white light over a red light in a vertical line

when the net is obstructed  - two red lights in a vertical line
